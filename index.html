RAID AWAKENINGS

Special Microchip • Fruit Awakening Effects • Sea 2 & Sea 3 locations

🔌

THE SPECIAL MICROCHIP

The Microchip is the key item needed to start a Fruit Awakening Raid. It costs around 1,000,000 Beli and is bought from the Raid NPC in Sea 3 (Port Town area). Rare drops also happen from high-level bosses and events.

Tip: You can only use 1 Microchip per raid. Bring friends — raids are much easier in a group of 2-4 players!
🌍

RAID LOCATIONS & ORDER

  • • Sea 2 — Easier raids for lower-rarity fruits (start here if you’re level 700+)
  • • Sea 3 — All raids available. Legendary & Mythical fruits require Sea 3 entry (Port Town Raid NPC)
  • • Recommended order: Common/Uncommon → Rare → Legendary → Mythical (hardest raids last)

The raid itself happens in a private dimension. You keep your current fruit equipped — the raid tests your ability to awaken it.

FRUIT AWAKENING EFFECTS

🐉DRAGON

Awakening: +40% damage, longer flight time, new ultimate fire transformation with massive AoE burn. Becomes the #1 PvP fruit.

🦊KITSUNE

Awakening: Stronger illusions, lightning storm now covers larger area, new tail whip combo. Perfect 1v1 meta.

🐆LEOPARD

Awakening: Insane speed boost, claws now have bleed effect, new predator leap that stuns. Dominates Sea 3.

🍩DOUGH

Awakening: Sticky traps last longer, giant form now has bigger hitbox and stun, new donut barrage.

☠️VENOM

Awakening: Poison DoT doubled, toxic fog now slows enemies, new serpent ultimate that poisons entire screen.

🧘BUDDHA

Awakening: Golden form lasts longer, bigger AoE palm strikes, massive defense boost for grinding.

🔥PHOENIX

Awakening: Healing flames now heal allies too, infinite flight with no stamina drain, rebirth ability revives you once.

🌋MAGMA

Awakening: Lava pools ignite faster, new eruption that launches enemies, huge burn damage over time.

⚡LIGHT

Awakening: Faster teleport dashes, laser beam pierces multiple enemies, light form now reflects projectiles.

❄️ICE

Awakening: Freeze duration doubled, ice platforms now damage enemies on contact, new glacial spike ultimate.

🔥FLAME

Awakening: Stronger burn, fireball barrage now homes in, flight speed greatly increased.

🌑DARK

Awakening: Black hole pulls stronger, new dark explosion that blinds enemies, better crowd control.

Pro tip: Only higher-rarity fruits (Rare+) have full awakenings. Common fruits like Rocket and Spin do not awaken. Bring Observation Haki and good blocks. Raids scale with your level and fruit.
