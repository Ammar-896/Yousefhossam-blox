RAIDS & FRUIT AWAKENINGS

Complete raids to unlock awakened moves • Requires level 1100+ • Sea 2 & Sea 3

🎫
How to Start a Raid
1. Be level 1100+ and reach Sea 2 or Sea 3
2. Find the Mysterious Scientist in the Factory (Sea 2: Hot & Cold Island, Sea 3: Castle on the Sea)
3. Buy a Raid Microchip for 100,000 Beli OR any physical fruit
4. Stand in the raid tubes/platforms and activate the chip
💡 Advanced Raids (Phoenix, Dough) cost 1,000 Fragments or a fruit worth 1M+
⚔️
Raid Mechanics
• 5 islands with increasingly difficult waves of NPCs
• Boss appears on final island
• 15 minute time limit
• Up to 4 players can join
• Earn 300-1,000 Fragments per completion
💡 You need to have the fruit EQUIPPED to awaken it after completing the raid

BLOX FRUITS HISTORY

From 2019 launch to 2026 — the full story of the biggest One Piece Roblox game

In 2026, Blox Fruits continues to thrive with frequent updates introducing new mythical fruits, fighting styles, and limited-time events. It remains one of the most popular and enduring experiences on Roblox. Created by the developer rip_indra and first released in 2019, the game draws heavy inspiration from One Piece. Players explore a vast open-world MMORPG, sail across multiple seas, consume powerful Devil Fruits that grant supernatural abilities, master unique combat styles, and battle enemies ranging from NPCs to high-level bosses. Over the years, the game has expanded significantly, adding new islands, advanced mechanics, and challenging end-game content. Major updates introduced systems like fruit awakenings and powerful fighting styles such as Godhuman and Sanguine Art, pushing gameplay depth even further. Later expansions brought Sea 3, advanced raids, and massive bosses like Leviathan. Throughout its history, Blox Fruits has surpassed billions of visits, solidifying its position as one of the most successful Roblox games ever. While it faced minor concerns due to its close resemblance to One Piece concepts, it has continued to grow without major disruption. One notable change was the removal of the Kilo Fruit, which was deemed too difficult to balance. It is now restricted to admin use and no longer obtainable by regular players. Today, Blox Fruits continues to evolve, consistently delivering fresh content and maintaining its status as the ultimate Roblox pirate adventure.
